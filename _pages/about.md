My name is Tien Cuong PHI, a Project Assistant Professor at Kyoto University, Japan (2025–present). Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Kyoto University (2023–2025), a University Teaching and Research Assistant at Université Côte d’Azur (formerly the University of Nice), France (2019–2022), and a Researcher at the Institute of Mathematics in Hanoi, Vietnam (2017–2020). I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Université Côte d’Azur (formerly the University of Nice) (2018–2022), following an M.Sc. in Probability and Statistics (2017–2018) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from Hanoi National University of Education (2012–2016).
